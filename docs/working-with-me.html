Working with me

My interests

Product development and design

  • I’m passionate about building and designing products that make people happy.
    • And I enjoy dissecting and analyzing great products too!
  • I’m obsessed with all parts of the design process from the high-level over-arching vision to UX strategy like how to design the UI to optimize conversions down to the nitty gritty details like how to make clicking a button on a form a delightful experience.

Engineering

  • I love prototyping and actualizing ideas into awesome products.
    • Bonus points if technical edge is required!

Growth & optimization

  • I’m addicted to seeing numbers go up and to the right, and even moreso with brainstorming and hacking together creative, out-of-the-box ways to do so.
  • I delight in the art of persuasion/negotiation and making things happen
    • If you’ve known me long enough, you may have experienced this first hand 🤣

Working style

Light validation and aggressive execution

  • I like moving fast and don’t like getting bogged down in extensive research. That’s not to say I don’t believe in any validation, but I’m a big believer that any idea can get fudded to death if you get too lost in the weeds. Let’s validate/settle on a general direction and just build shit.
  • I like to set aggressive deadlines, but this means that I can sometimes be overly optimistic!
    • Do call me out on this if you notice it 🙂

Quality

  • I work thoughtfully and deliberately and won’t deliver something until I can be proud of it, and I expect you to do the same.
    • Moving fast and being aggressive =/= sloppy work.
  • I don’t like half-assing things or sweeping things under the rug, especially when it comes to issues of legality e.g. copyright infringement, contractual obligations, etc.

Collaboration

  • I’m a very collaborative person, which means I like to discuss and debate decisions and bounce ideas around.
  • When I believe in a decision, I will advocate heavily for it, but welcome a challenging debate and am always open to changing my mind. My goal is not to “win” but rather make sure we arrive to the best conclusion.
    • Sometimes when I’m on the fence about something I’ll (passionately) play both sides of the argument. It might be confusing to see, but again I’m just trying to make sure we arrive to the best conclusion!
  • I enjoy working cross-functionally and strongly dislike being siloed in an individual function, so expect to see me taking point on things across multiple verticals
  • Similarly, I like giving input and having a say in all matters, even if it’s something I’m not directly working on (and I welcome input from you as well on things I’m working on).
    • Don’t take this as micro-management, I just like giving my perspective on things and hearing your perspective on things as well 🙂
    • I certainly still do expect you to make many judgment calls/decisions on your own. Just please discuss/keep me in the loop about high-level/big things (and I will too).
  • Accountability is important to me: if you say you’ll do something, I expect you to follow through and be responsible for it.

Transparency and openness

  • I like to be kept in the loop about everything about a project that I’m working on, even if it’s something that I’m not directly working on.
  • I will share equal access to everything I’m working on, e.g. code, dashboards, credentials for emails/social media accounts/etc, and I expect you to as well.
    • This allows us to get updates on what we’re working on on an as-needed basis without having to actively ask and wait for a response from someone else.
    • If you have something in particular you want to actively share or flag you definitely still should, though!
    • Please (B)CC me on any important emails with customers/vendors/etc.
  • Let’s keep meetings and syncs lightweight and to-the-point.

Show and tell frequently

  • I love sending progress pics/videos/demos and I love seeing them more.
  • This helps keep morale/motivation up and is also a great way to get micro-feedback and keep everyone up-to-date on state.

Work hard, play hard

  • Let’s be sure to keep our foot on the gas pedal and aggressively hit targets.
  • I want us to be so excited about what we’re working on that we don’t have a fixed mindset of weekdays/weekends. This doesn’t mean we should be grinding 24/7 eat sleep work repeat with no time for other activities, but rather that we enjoy the work so much that it’s what we want to spend each day doing by default.
  • Play hard: That being said, I think it’s completely reasonable if you want to block out time daily to go to the gym, go out on a weekend with friends, take some time off after a large milestone to chill, or work remotely from the Maldives for a week for a change in scenery (this one might be me projecting a bit…)
  • At the end of the day, I mainly want/need to be able to trust that we’re putting in an equally high level of effort and producing the same level of output (and this applies of course to me for you as well).

Feedback & conflict resolution

  • Please feel free to give feedback, positive or negative, about anything whenever.
  • If something bothers you, please bring it up ASAP—don’t let things sit.

Communication

Chat

  • This is my #1 preferred communication method by far.
  • I’m generally always available to discuss things or bounce ideas off of, and I like it when you are as well
  • You should feel free to send me a bump if it’s urgent and I haven’t responded yet or if you suspect that your message got lost.

Voice

  • If we’re not in-person, I’m a big fan of hanging out in a voice channel (e.g. a Discord channel) while working.
  • One of my pet peeves is being called unnecessarily (could this have just been a text?)

Email

  • If it’s communication with a third-party, something forwarded from a third-party, or it’s an archive of something, otherwise…
  • Please avoid 🙂
